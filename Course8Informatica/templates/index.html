Genes

Search results are examined for (possible new) genes, with the help of an algorithm.

Phenotypes

Search results are examined for phenotypes. It uses mesh terms to look for these phenotypes.

Time

A time frame can be entered, in which the tool searches.

You

You, the user, decides what other (textual) parameters you want to help you narrow down your search. The 'other' search field can be filled with any regular PubMed search term.

How does it work

Format your own PubMed search query with PubMole. PubMole offers different searchbars which compose your PubMed search query! Results are parsed and edited by our gene and phenotype identifiers to help you get all the relevant information.

Examining results

Examining results is easier then ever! Color codes for genes help you identify GenePanel-, NCBI- or even newer less known genes. But be aware, algorithms can make mistakes too!

Exporting results

All results can be exported to a csv file. Use the select buttons next to the titles in the results to select your relevant results!
